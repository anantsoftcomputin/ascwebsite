FAQ

1How do I create a product with you?

If you have an idea contact us by our online form, e-mail or phone shown in our website. We'll meet and talk it over. Just be sure to prepare as much info about your idea as possible, it will smoothen the meeting and benefit further cooperation.

2 Should I create a mobile or a web app?

We provide both and they have their benefits and flaws. Mobile apps are surely more expensive but can provide you with many more data collecting. Progressive Web Apps are a good compromise between a mobile and web app.You may need both mobile and web app and when that moment comes we are here for you :)

3What do I need to know before contacting you?

The most important thing to know is what do you want to accomplish. Why do I need this software? What for? What should it do? Having a clear vision in mind is crucial when ordering a software application. You don't want to spend many months developing it with us without being sure what you need.

4 How much time will it take for you to make my app?

We must prepare a project scope and create a budget. We shall tell you how much of that scope can be completed within the budget and we'll be able to estimate how long will it take after at least a month of work.

5How much do you charge?

It depends on many things: project scope, time spent on the project and technological issues. Remember that each software project is different, especially if you had a particularly original idea. We do not want to give you an unrealistic estimate, instead we want to provide you with the best quality possible within your budget.
